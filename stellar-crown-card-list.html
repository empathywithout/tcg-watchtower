Stellar Crown Card List

Scarlet & Violet

Complete guide to Stellar Crown (SV7) — full card list, chase cards ranked by market price, and where to buy sealed product.

Stellar Crown is the seventh Scarlet & Violet expansion, released September 13, 2024. Based on Japan's Stellar Miracle set, it introduced Stellar-type Tera Pokémon ex headlined by Terapagos ex. The 175-card set spans 142 main set cards plus 33 secret rares. The top chase cards are the Squirtle IR and Bulbasaur IR — rare Gen 1 starters with some of the most sought-after artwork in the set — alongside the Terapagos ex SIR and Dachsbun ex SIR. This complete Stellar Crown card list includes every card with rarity filters and live prices updated daily.

A complete Stellar Crown master set contains 317 cards — 175 main set and secret rare cards plus 142 reverse holos (one for each Common, Uncommon, Rare, and Double Rare). Secret rares do not have reverse holo variants.

Stellar Crown FAQ

What is the most expensive Stellar Crown card?

The most expensive Stellar Crown cards are the Squirtle Illustration Rare (#148) and Bulbasaur Illustration Rare (#143) — Gen 1 starters that consistently top the price charts. The Terapagos ex SIR (#170) and Dachsbun ex SIR (#169) are also top pulls. Check live prices on TCG Watchtower.

See all Stellar Crown chase cards ranked by price →

How many cards are in the Stellar Crown card list?

Stellar Crown contains 175 cards — 142 main set cards plus 33 secret rares including Illustration Rares and Special Illustration Rares.

When did Stellar Crown release?

Stellar Crown released September 13, 2024 as the seventh set in the Scarlet & Violet era.

What is the Stellar-type mechanic in Stellar Crown?

Stellar Crown introduced Stellar-type Tera Pokemon ex — a special rainbow type that can use any Energy — headlined by Terapagos ex as the iconic Stellar-type card.
