Twilight Masquerade Card List

Scarlet & Violet

Complete guide to Twilight Masquerade (SV6) — full card list, chase cards ranked by market price, and where to buy sealed product.

Twilight Masquerade is the sixth Scarlet & Violet expansion, released May 24, 2024. Based on Japan's Mask of Change set, it is built around Ogerpon ex in all four mask forms. The Greninja ex Special Illustration Rare (#214) is the top chase pull of the set — one of the most valuable cards in the Scarlet & Violet era, driven by stunning artwork and competitive playability. The Wellspring Mask Ogerpon ex SIR is also a strong pull. The 226-card set spans 162 main set cards plus 64 secret rares. This complete Twilight Masquerade card list includes every card with rarity filters and live prices updated daily.

A complete Twilight Masquerade master set contains 393 cards — 226 main set and secret rare cards plus 167 reverse holos (one for each Common, Uncommon, Rare, and Double Rare). Secret rares do not have reverse holo variants.

Twilight Masquerade FAQ

What is the most expensive Twilight Masquerade card?

The most expensive Twilight Masquerade card is the Greninja ex Special Illustration Rare (#214) — one of the most valuable cards in the entire Scarlet & Violet era, driven by stunning artwork and competitive playability. Wellspring Mask Ogerpon ex SIR and Perrin SIR are also strong pulls. Check live prices on TCG Watchtower.

See all Twilight Masquerade chase cards ranked by price →

How many cards are in the Twilight Masquerade card list?

Twilight Masquerade contains 226 cards — 162 main set cards plus 64 secret rares including Illustration Rares and Special Illustration Rares.

When did Twilight Masquerade release?

Twilight Masquerade released May 24, 2024 as the sixth set in the Scarlet & Violet era.

What Pokemon headline Twilight Masquerade?

Twilight Masquerade is built around Ogerpon ex in all four mask forms — Hearthflame, Cornerstone, Wellspring, and Teal Mask — as the defining theme of the set.
