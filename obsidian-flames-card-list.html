Obsidian Flames Card List

Scarlet & Violet

Complete guide to Obsidian Flames (SV3) — full card list, chase cards ranked by market price, and where to buy sealed product.

Obsidian Flames is the third Scarlet & Violet expansion, released August 11, 2023. Based on Japan's Ruler of the Black Flame set, it introduced Tera-type Pokémon ex in multiple types across the set. The headline card is Charizard ex SIR — depicting Charizard in its black-and-gold Tera Dragon form — one of the most valuable and iconic cards of the Scarlet & Violet era. The 230-card set also features Tyranitar ex, Revavroom ex, and a strong Trainer SIR lineup. This complete Obsidian Flames card list includes every card with rarity filters and live prices updated daily.

A complete Obsidian Flames master set contains 427 cards — 230 main set and secret rare cards plus 197 reverse holos (one for each Common, Uncommon, Rare, and Double Rare). Secret rares do not have reverse holo variants.

Obsidian Flames FAQ

What is the most expensive Obsidian Flames card?

The most expensive Obsidian Flames card is the Charizard ex Special Illustration Rare — a Tera Dragon-type Charizard ex with black-and-gold artwork that became one of the most iconic cards of the Scarlet & Violet era.

See all Obsidian Flames chase cards ranked by price →

How many cards are in the Obsidian Flames card list?

Obsidian Flames contains 230 cards — 151 main set cards plus 79 secret rares including Illustration Rares and Special Illustration Rares.

When did Obsidian Flames release?

Obsidian Flames released August 11, 2023 as the third set in the Scarlet & Violet era.

What is the Tera Charizard ex card?

The Charizard ex SIR in Obsidian Flames depicts a black-and-gold Tera Dragon Charizard. It is one of the most desirable Charizard cards ever printed and commands premium secondary market prices.
