Phantasmal Flames Card List

Mega Evolution

Complete guide to Phantasmal Flames — full card list, chase cards ranked by market price, and where to buy sealed product.

Phantasmal Flames is the second Mega Evolution expansion, released November 14, 2025. Based on Japan's Inferno X set plus the Mega Gengar ex and Mega Diancie ex Starter Sets, it features six Mega Evolution Pokémon ex: Mega Charizard X ex (the central card), Mega Gengar ex, Mega Heracross ex, Mega Lopunny ex, Mega Sharpedo ex, and Mega Diancie ex. The set focuses on Fire and Darkness types. Mega Charizard X ex SIR is the top chase pull in the set. The Dawn SIR and Mega Sharpedo ex SIR are also strong pulls. This complete Phantasmal Flames card list includes every card with rarity filters and live prices updated daily.

A complete Phantasmal Flames master set contains 224 cards — 130 main set and secret rare cards plus 94 reverse holos (one for each Common, Uncommon, Rare, and Double Rare). Secret rares do not have reverse holo variants.

Phantasmal Flames FAQ

What is the most expensive Phantasmal Flames card?

The most expensive Phantasmal Flames cards are the Mega Charizard X ex Special Illustration Rare and the Mega Charizard X ex Mega Hyper Rare gold card — the standout chase pulls of the set. The Dawn SIR and Mega Sharpedo ex SIR are also top pulls. Note: Mega Gengar ex SIR is from Ascended Heroes (ME2.5), not Phantasmal Flames. Check live prices on TCG Watchtower.

See all Phantasmal Flames chase cards ranked by price →

How many cards are in the Phantasmal Flames card list?

Phantasmal Flames contains 130 cards — 94 main set cards plus 36 secret rares including 13 Illustration Rares, 17 Ultra Rares, and 5 Special Illustration Rares.

When did Phantasmal Flames release?

Phantasmal Flames released November 14, 2025 as the second main expansion in the Pokemon TCG Mega Evolution series.

What Mega Pokemon are in Phantasmal Flames?

Phantasmal Flames features six Mega Evolution Pokemon ex: Mega Charizard X ex (the central card), Mega Gengar ex, Mega Heracross ex, Mega Lopunny ex, Mega Sharpedo ex, and Mega Diancie ex. The set focuses on Fire-type and Darkness-type Pokemon.

What Japanese set is Phantasmal Flames based on?

Phantasmal Flames is based on Japan's Inferno X expansion combined with the Mega Gengar ex and Mega Diancie ex Starter Sets.
