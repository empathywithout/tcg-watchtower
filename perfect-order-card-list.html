Perfect Order Card List

Mega Evolution

Complete guide to Perfect Order (ME3) — full card list, chase cards ranked by market price, and where to buy sealed product.

Perfect Order is the third main set in the Pokemon TCG Mega Evolution series, released in March 2026. The ME3 set contains 117 cards and is built around Mega Starmie ex, Mega Zygarde ex, and Mega Clefable ex as its headline Pokemon. The top chase cards include multiple Special Illustration Rares and a Mega Ultra Rare in Mega Zygarde ex at number 117, which sits at the apex of the pull sheet. Rosa's Encouragement has emerged as a standout Supporter card driving collector demand. This complete Perfect Order card list includes all ME3 cards with rarity labels, live prices updated daily on TCG Watchtower, and rarity filters so you can quickly locate any card in the set whether you are tracking a recent pull or researching values before buying.

A complete Perfect Order master set contains 212 cards — 124 main set and secret rare cards plus 88 reverse holos (one for each Common, Uncommon, Rare, and Double Rare). Secret rares do not have reverse holo variants.

Perfect Order FAQ

What is the most expensive Perfect Order card?

The most expensive Perfect Order card is the Mega Zygarde ex Mega Ultra Rare (#117). Rosa's Encouragement SIR is also a standout chase pull.

See all Perfect Order chase cards ranked by price →

How many cards are in the Perfect Order card list?

Perfect Order contains 124 cards — 81 main set cards plus secret rares across Illustration Rare, Ultra Rare, Special Illustration Rare, and Mega Ultra Rare tiers.

When did Perfect Order release?

Perfect Order released March 2026 as the third set in the Pokemon TCG Mega Evolution series.

What Mega Pokemon are in Perfect Order?

Perfect Order is headlined by Mega Zygarde ex, Mega Starmie ex, and Mega Clefable ex as its three flagship Mega Evolution Pokemon ex.
