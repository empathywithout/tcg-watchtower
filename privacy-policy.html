Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026  ·  Effective immediately

TCG Watchtower is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding your data.

01 — INFORMATION WE COLLECT

What We Collect

When you interact with TCG Watchtower, we may collect the following types of information:

  • Discord Account Data: When you join our Discord server, we receive your Discord username, user ID, and profile information as permitted by Discord's platform.
  • Usage Data: We collect information about how you interact with our services, including alert preferences, store selections, and command usage within our Discord bot.
  • Communication Data: If you contact us directly, we may retain records of that communication.
  • Website Analytics: Standard anonymised analytics data may be collected when you visit our website (e.g. page views, browser type, general location).

We do not collect payment information, passwords, or sensitive personal data beyond what is described above.

02 — HOW WE USE YOUR INFORMATION

Use of Data

We use the information we collect solely to operate and improve TCG Watchtower services:

  • Delivering restock alerts and notifications to your Discord account
  • Personalising alert preferences based on your selected stores and products
  • Monitoring and improving the performance of our alert systems
  • Responding to support requests and community feedback
  • Enforcing our community guidelines and Terms of Service

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with third-party advertisers.

03 — DISCORD & THIRD-PARTY SERVICES

Third-Party Platforms

Our primary service operates through Discord. By joining TCG Watchtower's Discord server or using our bot, you are also subject to Discord's Privacy Policy. We have no control over Discord's data practices.

Our website may contain links to retailer websites (Target, Walmart, Amazon, etc.). We are not responsible for the privacy practices of any external sites.

04 — DATA RETENTION & SECURITY

How We Protect Your Data

We retain your data only as long as necessary to provide our services. Alert preferences and bot configuration data are stored securely and deleted upon request or when you leave our Discord server.

We implement reasonable security measures to protect your data from unauthorised access. However, no system is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

05 — YOUR RIGHTS

Your Rights & Choices

You have the right to:

  • Request access to the personal data we hold about you
  • Request deletion of your data at any time
  • Opt out of non-essential data collection
  • Leave our Discord server at any time, which ends active data collection

To exercise any of these rights, contact us via our Discord server or at the contact information below.

06 — CHILDREN'S PRIVACY

Age Requirements

TCG Watchtower is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us and we will delete it promptly.

All users must comply with Discord's minimum age requirements (13+ in most regions).
