Frequently Asked Questions

Is TCG Watchtower really free?+
Yes! Joining our Discord and receiving restock alerts from major retailers is completely free. Some features, like in-store stock checkers, are part of our premium offerings, but core alerts will always remain free.
Which stores do you monitor?+
We monitor major retailers including Target, Walmart, Amazon, Best Buy, GameStop, Pokémon Center, and more. We're constantly adding new stores based on community feedback.
How fast are the alerts?+
Get 24/7 live alerts from major retailers, including Pokémon Center. Be notified the moment products go live, even before links appear, and stay ahead with instant alerts when a queue is active.
Can I customize which alerts I receive?+
Absolutely! Choose which stores you want to get alerts from.
