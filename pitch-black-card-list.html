Complete guide to Pitch Black — full card list, chase cards ranked by market price, and where to buy sealed product.

Pitch Black is the fifth set in the Pokemon TCG Mega Evolution series, releasing July 2026. Based on the Japanese Abyss Eye set, ME5 is headlined by Mega Darkrai ex as the top chase pull alongside Mega Zeraora ex, Mega Chandelure ex, and Mega Excadrill ex. This page currently shows the Japanese Abyss Eye card list — English names and prices will be added when Pitch Black releases.

A complete Pitch Black master set contains 200 cards — 118 main set and secret rare cards plus 82 reverse holos (one for each Common, Uncommon, Rare, and Double Rare). Secret rares do not have reverse holo variants.

Pitch Black FAQ

What is the most expensive Pitch Black card?

The most expensive Pitch Black card is the Mega Darkrai ex Special Illustration Rare, illustrated by Akira Egawa. Mega Zeraora ex SIR and Gwynn SIR are also top chase pulls.

See all Pitch Black chase cards ranked by price →

How many cards are in the Pitch Black card list?

Pitch Black contains 118 cards — 81 main set cards plus 37 secret rares including Illustration Rares, Special Illustration Rares, and the Mega Darkrai ex Mega Hyper Rare at #118.

When does Pitch Black release?

Pitch Black releases July 26, 2026. Prerelease events begin July 4, 2026 at participating local game stores.

What Mega Pokemon are in Pitch Black?

Pitch Black features four confirmed Mega Evolution Pokemon ex: Mega Darkrai ex, Mega Zeraora ex, Mega Chandelure ex, and Mega Excadrill ex.

Is Pitch Black based on a Japanese set?

Yes — Pitch Black is the English adaptation of the Japanese set Abyss Eye, released May 22, 2026. The sets are nearly identical.

What is the Pitch Black set code?

The Pitch Black set code is ME05, the fifth expansion in the Pokemon TCG Mega Evolution series.
