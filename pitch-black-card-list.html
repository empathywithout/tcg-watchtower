Pitch Black FAQ

What is the most expensive Pitch Black card?

The most expensive Pitch Black card is expected to be the Mega Darkrai ex Special Illustration Rare, illustrated by Akira Egawa. Based on its Japanese counterpart in Abyss Eye, it is the top chase pull of the set. Mega Zeraora ex SIR and Gwynn SIR are also expected to command strong prices.

How many cards are in the Pitch Black card list?

Pitch Black contains 118 cards in total — 81 main set cards plus 37 secret rares including Illustration Rares, Special Illustration Rares, Ultra Rares, and the Mega Darkrai ex Mega Hyper Rare at #118.

When does Pitch Black release?

Pitch Black releases July 26, 2026. Prerelease events begin July 4, 2026 at participating local game stores, giving collectors early access to booster packs before the official launch.

What Mega Pokemon are in Pitch Black?

Pitch Black features four confirmed Mega Evolution Pokemon ex: Mega Darkrai ex, Mega Zeraora ex, Mega Chandelure ex, and Mega Excadrill ex. Mega Darkrai ex is the headline card and primary chase target.

Is Pitch Black based on a Japanese set?

Yes — Pitch Black is the English adaptation of the Japanese set Abyss Eye, released May 22, 2026. The English set is a near 1:1 translation with the same card list, artwork, and rarities.

What is the Pitch Black set code?

The Pitch Black set code is ME05, as it is the fifth expansion in the Pokémon TCG Mega Evolution series.
