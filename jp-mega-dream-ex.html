MEGA Dream ex (M2a) is the High Class Pack subset of the Japanese Pokémon TCG Mega Evolution series, released November 28, 2025. As the Japanese source for the English Ascended Heroes (ME2.5), MEGA Dream ex is a reprint-heavy premium set focused entirely on Mega Evolution Pokémon ex including new Mega Evolution cards based on Pokémon Legends: Z-A. Each booster pack contains 10 cards with a guaranteed Pokémon ex or Mega Evolution Pokémon ex. MEGA Dream ex (M2a) FAQ

What is MEGA Dream ex?

MEGA Dream ex (M2a) is the Japanese High Class Pack subset in the Pokémon TCG Mega Evolution series, released November 28, 2025. It is the Japanese source for the English Ascended Heroes (ME2.5).

What is the English equivalent of MEGA Dream ex?

MEGA Dream ex (M2a) is the Japanese source for Ascended Heroes (ME2.5), which released in English in January 2026.

How is MEGA Dream ex different from other Mega Evolution sets?

MEGA Dream ex is a High Class Pack — each booster pack contains 10 cards (vs 5 in standard sets) and each box contains only 10 packs. Every pack guarantees at least one Pokémon ex or Mega Evolution Pokémon ex.

How many cards are in MEGA Dream ex?

MEGA Dream ex contains 250 cards in total, making it one of the largest Japanese sets in the Mega Evolution series.

Where can I buy MEGA Dream ex Japanese booster boxes?

MEGA Dream ex Japanese booster boxes are available on TCGplayer (product ID 666254), Amazon, and eBay. Each box contains 10 packs of 10 cards.
