White Flare Card List

Scarlet & Violet

Complete guide to White Flare — full card list, chase cards ranked by market price, and where to buy sealed product.

White Flare is one half of the SV10.5 split expansion, released July 18, 2025 alongside Black Bolt. Together they cover all 156 Unova Pokémon — White Flare focuses on Fire and Water types centered around Reshiram ex and Keldeo ex. Every Pokémon has an Art Rare or SIR variant. The ultra-rare Red Victini Black & White Rare is the rarest pull in the set. This complete White Flare card list includes every card with rarity filters and live prices updated daily.

A complete White Flare master set contains 260 cards — 173 main set and secret rare cards plus 87 reverse holos (one for each Common, Uncommon, Rare, and Double Rare). Secret rares do not have reverse holo variants.

White Flare FAQ

What is the most expensive White Flare card?

The most expensive White Flare cards are the Red Victini Black & White Rare, Reshiram ex SIR, and Hydreigon ex SIR. Red Victini is the rarest pull in the set.

See all White Flare chase cards ranked by price →

How many cards are in the White Flare card list?

White Flare contains 173 cards — 86 main set cards plus 87 secret rares including Art Rares, Special Illustration Rares, and Black & White Rares.

When did White Flare release?

White Flare released July 18, 2025 alongside Black Bolt as the SV10.5 split Unova expansion.

Does White Flare have God Packs?

Yes — White Flare features God Packs containing 9 Illustration Rares and 1 Special Illustration Rare.
