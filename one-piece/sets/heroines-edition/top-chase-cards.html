Frequently Asked Questions

What are the chase cards in Heroines Edition?

The chase cards in Heroines Edition are its highest-rarity pulls — Manga Rare, Secret Rare, Alternate Art, Special, Super Rare. These are the cards collectors specifically hope to pull from a booster box, and the main driver of a box's resale value.

What is the most valuable Heroines Edition card?

Uta (mangaAltArt) is typically the most valuable Heroines Edition pull, as a Manga Rare card. See live pricing for it and every other chase card ranked below.

How many chase cards are in Heroines Edition?

39 out of 90 total cards in Heroines Edition qualify as chase-tier rarities.
