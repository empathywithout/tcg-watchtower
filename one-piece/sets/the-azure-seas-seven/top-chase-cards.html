The chase cards in The Azure Sea's Seven are its highest-rarity pulls — Manga Rare, Secret Rare, Treasure Rare, Alternate Art, Special, Super Rare. These are the cards collectors specifically hope to pull from a booster box, and the main driver of a box's resale value.

What is the most valuable The Azure Sea's Seven card?

Dracule Mihawk (mangaAltArt) is typically the most valuable The Azure Sea's Seven pull, as a Manga Rare card. See live pricing for it and every other chase card ranked below.
