Frequently Asked Questions

What are the chase cards in The Time of Battle?

The chase cards in The Time of Battle are its highest-rarity pulls — Manga Rare, Secret Rare, Treasure Rare, Alternate Art, Special, Super Rare. These are the cards collectors specifically hope to pull from a booster box, and the main driver of a box's resale value.

What is the most valuable The Time of Battle card?

Kuzan (mangaAltArt) is typically the most valuable The Time of Battle pull, as a Manga Rare card. See live pricing for it and every other chase card ranked below.
