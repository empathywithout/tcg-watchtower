Frequently Asked Questions

What are the chase cards in Perfect Order?

The chase cards in Perfect Order are its highest-rarity pulls — Mega Hyper Rare, Special Illustration Rare, Ultra Rare, Illustration Rare. These are the cards collectors specifically hope to pull from a booster box, and the main driver of a box's resale value.

What is the most valuable Perfect Order card?

Mega Zygarde ex is typically the most valuable Perfect Order pull, as a Mega Hyper Rare card. See live pricing for it and every other chase card ranked below.

How many chase cards are in Perfect Order?

36 out of 124 total cards in Perfect Order qualify as chase-tier rarities.
