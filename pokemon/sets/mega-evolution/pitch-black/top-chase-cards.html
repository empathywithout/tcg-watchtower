Frequently Asked Questions

What are the chase cards in Pitch Black?

The chase cards in Pitch Black are its highest-rarity pulls — Mega Hyper Rare, Special Illustration Rare, Ultra Rare, Illustration Rare. These are the cards collectors specifically hope to pull from a booster box, and the main driver of a box's resale value.

What is the most valuable Pitch Black card?

Mega Darkrai ex is typically the most valuable Pitch Black pull, as a Mega Hyper Rare card. See live pricing for it and every other chase card ranked below.

How many chase cards are in Pitch Black?

36 out of 120 total cards in Pitch Black qualify as chase-tier rarities.
