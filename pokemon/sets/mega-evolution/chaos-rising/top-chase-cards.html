What are the chase cards in Chaos Rising?

The chase cards in Chaos Rising are its highest-rarity pulls — Mega Hyper Rare, Special Illustration Rare, Ultra Rare, Illustration Rare. These are the cards collectors specifically hope to pull from a booster box, and the main driver of a box's resale value.

What is the most valuable Chaos Rising card?

Mega Greninja ex is typically the most valuable Chaos Rising pull, as a Mega Hyper Rare card. See live pricing for it and every other chase card ranked below.

How many chase cards are in Chaos Rising?
