What are the chase cards in Stellar Crown?

The chase cards in Stellar Crown are its highest-rarity pulls — Hyper Rare, Special Illustration Rare, Ultra Rare, Illustration Rare. These are the cards collectors specifically hope to pull from a booster box, and the main driver of a box's resale value.

What is the most valuable Stellar Crown card?

Area Zero Underdepths is typically the most valuable Stellar Crown pull, as a Hyper Rare card. See live pricing for it and every other chase card ranked below.

How many chase cards are in Stellar Crown?
