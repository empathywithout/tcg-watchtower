Frequently Asked Questions

What are the chase cards in Surging Sparks?

The chase cards in Surging Sparks are its highest-rarity pulls — Hyper Rare, Special Illustration Rare, Ultra Rare, Illustration Rare. These are the cards collectors specifically hope to pull from a booster box, and the main driver of a box's resale value.

What is the most valuable Surging Sparks card?

Alolan Exeggutor ex is typically the most valuable Surging Sparks pull, as a Hyper Rare card. See live pricing for it and every other chase card ranked below.

How many chase cards are in Surging Sparks?

61 out of 252 total cards in Surging Sparks qualify as chase-tier rarities.
