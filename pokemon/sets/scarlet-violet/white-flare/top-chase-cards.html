Frequently Asked Questions

What are the chase cards in White Flare?

The chase cards in White Flare are its highest-rarity pulls — Special Illustration Rare, Ultra Rare, Illustration Rare. These are the cards collectors specifically hope to pull from a booster box, and the main driver of a box's resale value.

What is the most valuable White Flare card?

Bouffalant ex is typically the most valuable White Flare pull, as a Special Illustration Rare card. See live pricing for it and every other chase card ranked below.

How many chase cards are in White Flare?

84 out of 173 total cards in White Flare qualify as chase-tier rarities.
