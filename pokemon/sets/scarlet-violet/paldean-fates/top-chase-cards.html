Frequently Asked Questions

What are the chase cards in Paldean Fates?

The chase cards in Paldean Fates are its highest-rarity pulls — Hyper Rare, Special Illustration Rare, Ultra Rare, Illustration Rare. These are the cards collectors specifically hope to pull from a booster box, and the main driver of a box's resale value.

What is the most valuable Paldean Fates card?

Chi-Yu ex is typically the most valuable Paldean Fates pull, as a Hyper Rare card. See live pricing for it and every other chase card ranked below.

How many chase cards are in Paldean Fates?

22 out of 245 total cards in Paldean Fates qualify as chase-tier rarities.
